👋 Introduction

Welcome to ItemGraze Content Scheduler ("ItemGraze," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information with transparency and care.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our content scheduling and management service. By using ItemGraze, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Content data: Images, titles, descriptions, and scheduling preferences you upload or create within ItemGraze.
  • Platform connection data: OAuth tokens required to connect your accounts on supported platforms (e.g., Pinterest). We never store your platform passwords.
  • Usage data: Basic usage logs to help us diagnose errors and improve the service.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government-issued identification, health data, or precise geolocation.

🔧 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve ItemGraze:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable content scheduling and publishing features
  • To connect to third-party platforms on your behalf, using only the permissions you grant
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To maintain the security and reliability of our service

We only use your data for the purposes described above. We do not use your data for advertising targeting, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the ItemGraze service.

🔒 We Do Not Sell or Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With platforms you connect: When you explicitly authorize ItemGraze to publish content to a platform (e.g., Pinterest), we transmit the content you've selected to that platform on your behalf.
  • With service providers: We may use trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting) who process data only under our instructions and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law: We may disclose data if required by valid legal process.

🎛️ You Control Your Content

Your content belongs to you. ItemGraze does not claim ownership over any images, text, or media you upload or create using our service.

  • You can edit or delete your content at any time from within the app.
  • You can disconnect any linked platform account at any time, which will revoke ItemGraze's access to that platform.
  • You can request deletion of your ItemGraze account and all associated data by contacting us at support@itemgraze.com.

🛡️ Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Platform access tokens are stored encrypted and are never exposed in plaintext.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at support@itemgraze.com.

🍪 Cookies

ItemGraze uses only essential cookies required to maintain your session and keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice in the application before the changes take effect. Continued use of ItemGraze after an update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
